Jula Pavić

You want to know how to paint a perfect painting? It’s easy. Make yourself perfect and then just paint naturally. That’s the way all the experts do it. The making of a painting is very much like the making of a motorcycle. The difference is only in the kind of tools. In the hands of an unskilled person, a paintbrush becomes just a stick with some hairs on the end. In the hands of an artist, it becomes a conduit of vision and emotion. Same with a wrench. Same with life. And what’s at the heart of this transformation? Quality. Not just a word or a measure, but an event. An experience. Something that happens between the subject and the object. Something that can't be defined, only recognized. You know it when it’s there. And you know it when it’s gone.
